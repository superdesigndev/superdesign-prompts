## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Print-editorial / luxury-magazine aesthetic translated to the web: warm cream paper canvas, near-black ink, and ONE muted burgundy accent. The voice is the typography itself — an oversized optical serif (Fraunces) for the statement and links set against quiet Inter sans for nav, labels and body. No buttons, cards, gradients or shadows; the only ornament is hairline rules, animated text underlines, and a faint radial dot-grain. Tight negative leading and negative tracking on the display serif give it a confident masthead feel; uppercase, wide-tracked micro-labels do the editorial signposting.
